The Long Night


Welcome to The Long Night Simulator!

A simple game with one clear mission: Survive the waves of undead hordes as long as you can.

Basic Controls:

  • W: Move Up
  • A: Move Left
  • S: Move Down
  • D: Move Right
  • O: Swing Sword
  • P: Use Blood Orb

There's a catch to all this though. As you grow more powerful, and slaughter those who have been slaughtered before, be aware: the more you use your strength and power ups, the weaker you become. Eventually, the undead will overtake you. But just as inevitably, you, yes you reading this, will be overtaken by the sweet release of death. All we can say to the god of death, both here and afk is one thing- "Not Today"
